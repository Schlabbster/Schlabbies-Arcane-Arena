Quick FAQ

What is this page?

A lightweight landing page that helps people discover my VTT/TTRPG tools via search and share links. Each tile opens a project instantly in a new tab.

What VTTs does this support?

Anything that accepts images, handouts, or videos (Foundry VTT, Roll20, Owlbear Rodeo, etc.). Export from the tools and drop the assets into your workflow.

Are these tools free?

Yes! All tools in the Arcane Arena are completely free to use. I built them to share with the community and support my own creative projects. If you find them useful, consider supporting on Ko-fi or sharing with your gaming group!

Can I contribute or suggest features?

Absolutely! I'm open to feedback, suggestions, and contributions. You can reach out on Discord (@Schlabbie) or submit ideas on GitHub. If you have a feature request or want to collaborate, don't hesitate to get in touch!

How do I use the tools?

Each tool has its own interface and instructions, but generally, you can customize parameters, generate content, and export assets directly from the web app. The Arcane Card Engine lets you build encounter and story prompts using modular decks. The Sigil Generator creates procedural magical symbols with export options. The NPC Foundry generates fully fleshed-out characters with names, stats, and backstories. Just click on a tile to open the tool and start creating!

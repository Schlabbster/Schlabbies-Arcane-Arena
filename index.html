Arcane Card Engine (Alpha)

Story Hooks/Encounters

The Arcane Card Engine is a browser-based fantasy encounter and plot-/storyhook generator for TTRPGs and VTT play. It creates structured story prompts using modular Major & Minor decks, themes, archetypes, and optional reversals. Designed for fast inspiration, session prep, and expandable narrative worldbuilding.

Prompts • Plothooks • Encounters Open tool

Arcane Sigil Generator

Sigils

Procedural Sigil Generator is a lightweight, browser-based tool for creating animated arcane seals and magical glyphs. Exports include PNG, GIF, and MP4, making it ideal for VTT spell assets and TTRPG visuals. It lets users control templates, symmetry, complexity, text rings, and layered effects with deterministic seeds.

FAQ

What is Schlabbies Arcane Arena?

It’s a lightweight hub page that links to my browser-based TTRPG tools - currently the Arcane Card Engine and the Magic Sigil Generator - designed for VTT and tabletop use.

Are these tools free to use?

Yes - they’re hosted on GitHub Pages and meant to be easy to access and share with your group. If you’d like to support development, you can do so via Ko-fi below.

What virtual tabletops work with these?

Anything that accepts images/handouts/MP4 (for example Foundry VTT, Roll20, Owlbear Rodeo, etc.). Export from the tools and use the assets in your preferred VTT workflow.
